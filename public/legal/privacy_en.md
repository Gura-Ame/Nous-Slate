# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 2025**

How we handle your data.

## 1. Data Collection

When you sign in with Google, we collect your Email, display name, and avatar URL. We store your learning records, points transactions, and subscription content.

## 2. Data Usage

Data is used solely to provide personalized learning services (such as SRS algorithm scheduling) and sync your device progress.

## 3. Data Security

This service uses Google Firebase for data storage. Your data is protected by Google's security infrastructure. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 4. Cookies

This service uses Cookies and LocalStorage to maintain login status and preference settings.

## 5. User Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all related data at any time.
